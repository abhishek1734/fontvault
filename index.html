Frequently Asked Questions

What is FontVault?

FontVault is a curated typography platform that allows designers to explore, test, and pair premium and free typefaces inside real-world design mockups.

How does the AI Font Pairing Studio work?

The AI Font Pairing Studio analyzes the design parameters, tone, and style tags of your project description using Gemini AI to recommend harmonious pairing combinations.

Are the fonts on FontVault free to use?

We feature a mix of open-source, free for personal use, and commercial typefaces. Each font detail page lists the designer, foundry, and license type.

How do I download a font?

Simply click the download button on any font specimen page. FontVault proxies and packages the files directly for quick deployment.
